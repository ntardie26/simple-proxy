About Simple Web Proxy

Our Mission

Simple Web Proxy was created with one goal in mind: provide a straightforward way to access the web without restrictions, tracking, or unnecessary features. We believe internet access should be simple, private, and available to everyone.

What Makes Us Different

Unlike other proxy services that bombard you with ads, collect your data, or limit your browsing capabilities, Simple Web Proxy focuses on what matters most: a clean, fast browsing experience with robust privacy features.

  • Minimalist Design - No distractions, just the content you want
  • Privacy-Focused - Built with your digital privacy as a priority
  • Open Source - Transparent code anyone can review and contribute to
  • No User Tracking - We don't collect or store any of your browsing data

How It Works

Simple Web Proxy acts as an intermediary between you and the websites you visit. When you enter a URL or search query, our servers fetch the content for you, allowing you to browse websites that might otherwise be restricted in your location or network.

Looking Forward

We're constantly working to improve Simple Web Proxy with new features and optimizations. If you have suggestions or want to contribute, check out our GitHub repository.
